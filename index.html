About us

Some words about us

Entrepreneurship-Cell is a non-profit organization run by the students of M.S.I.T. It seeks to foster the spirit of Entrepreneurship amongst aspirant entrepreneurs by exposing them to the joys and challenges of having their own ventures.

  • Mission - We are on a mission to promote entrepreneurship through talks, events and hands-on experience at MSIT.

Take a look at WHAT WE DO

Founded in 2013, EDC has seen substantial growth and has been recognised as the “Leading Entrepreneurship Promoting Student Organisation in the College”

ETalks

The EDC Talks are a series of interactive talks given by the founders and top executives of startups to a group of MSIT students.

StartVenture

If possible start a venture on campus to get first hand experience of starting up. If that might seem a little difficult, one / more groups can do a pilot run to get a better idea of how real life micro-businesses work.

Workshops

Workshops relating to Start-up skills(Finance, Marketing, IP issues, Seed-Funding opportunities via Investor Pitchs, Help getting incubated/accelerated somewhere).

Career Fair

EDC connects the most innovative companies in the city with top engineering, business and marketing talent at MSIT. Our external relations team is currently in the process of planning the 2016 EDC Startup Career Fair.
